Charging Station

If you are a real Hipster you want to reduce environmental damage as much as you can. Humanity is destroying our wonderful planet earth and so we Hipsters have to step up! Although traveling by public transport is the most sustainable way, we can now also drive in affordable cars to be electrically charged. Starbucks Coffee

We Hipsters know about Starbucks’ awesome CSR policies and have been supportive since day one. But have you heard about Starbucks’ new initiative called ‘Upstanders’ already? It is a new original series featuring a collection of 10 short films, essays, and podcasts that share the stories of ordinary Americans doing extraordinary things to create positive change in their communities. This effort reminds us that we all have the power to make a difference. So be a Hipster and share your Starbucks!

Tattoos

As you have probably noticed, wannabe Hipsters are now trying to copy our style. They hype over fake ear plugs, fake dreadlocks and fake iPhones. Nowadays they even fake over tattoos and most importantly: they copied our Mother Hipster. The best way to deal with these ‘Wipsters’ is to ignore them, like all other non-Hipsters. Let’s praise Mother Hipster and hope this hype will blow away soon…
